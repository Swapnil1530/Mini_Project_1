Syllabus Content  Download

Unit-1: Fundamental of Computer Graphics

Basic of Computer Graphics, Applications of computer graphics, Video Display devices, Random and Raster scan systems, Graphics Software.

Unit-2: Graphics Primitives

Points, lines, circles and ellipses, Filled-area primitives, Cell array, Character generation, Line attributes, Area-fill attributes, Character attributers, Antialiasing.

Unit-3: 2D transformation

Basic Transformations techniques, Matrix representation, Homogeneous coordinates, Composite transformations, Reflection and Shearing

Unit-4: 2D viewing

The viewing pipeline and coordinates system, Window-toviewport transformation, Clipping including point clipping, Line clipping - Cohen-Sutherland, Liang- Bersky, Nicholl-Lee-Nicholl, Polygon clippingSutherland-Hodgeman polygon clipping, Weiler-Atherton polygon clipping, text clipping, exterior clipping

Unit-5:  3D Modeling & Projection

: 3D display methods, 3D scaling, rotation and translation, reflection & shears, parallel projection, perspective projection. Curve & Surface: Polygon surfaces, curved lies and surfaces, quadric surfaces, spline representation, cubic spline interpolation methods, overview of Bazier curves and surfaces.

Unit-6: Fundamental of visible surface detection

back-face detection, depth buffer method, A-buffer method, scan-line method, depth-sorting method, BSP-Tree method illumination, light sources, basic illumination model, Color modelsproperties of light, XYZ, RGB, YIQ and CMY color models. Current Trend Computer Graphics - Animations & Realism, Augmented reality, Case Study.

Course Outcome

After learning the course the students should be able to:

  • To understand basic concepts of computer graphics
  • To understand the methods of problem solving in the domain of computer graphics.
  • Understand basic concepts related to 2D and 3D graphics algorithms in developing various applications.
  •  Understand various aspects of computer visualization and color model
  • After completion of this course student will able to Design animation sequences.
